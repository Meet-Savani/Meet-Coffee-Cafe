About Us

What Makes Our Coffee Special?

There's a lot more to specialty coffee than meets the eye. If there's one thing it has going for it, it's the quality. You'll be shocked if you go to a coffee shop and watch a good, experienced barista at work.

You'll note that the barista uses a scale to weigh the coffee beans, grinds them, and pours them using a special technique. Observing how a barista prepares a specialty coffee is one thing; experiencing the aroma and taste of the specialty coffee is quite another. Whatever you select, you'll experience something unlike any other, with its rich flavor notes and robust scent.
